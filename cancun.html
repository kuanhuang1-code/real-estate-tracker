No reservations needed. No limits on ordering. Just incredible food from around the world, whenever you want it. People rave about the food here — it's NOT your typical all-inclusive buffet.

🇫🇷 Chez Isabelle

French Fine Dining

The famous duck! Plus lobster, foie gras, scallops, filet mignon. Live music makes it ultra romantic.

🦞 The Lobster House

Seafood & Shellfish

Order as many lobsters as you want — grilled or steamed with 5 sauce options. Octopus, ceviche, mussels...

Italian

Hand-rolled pasta, slow-simmered sauces. Also has the best breakfast buffet — fresh juices, omelettes, pastries!

Mexican

Authentic Mexican dishes with live mariachi band. Plus Friday Fiesta Mexicana with street tacos & churros!

🇮🇳 Basmati

Indian

Creamy curries, fluffy naan, tikka masala, vindaloo lamb. Trained chefs who specialize in Indian cuisine.

Cured meats, artisanal cheeses, wine pairings. The octopus and patatas bravas are incredible.
