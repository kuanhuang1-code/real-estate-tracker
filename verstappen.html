💥 Notable Crashes

🇦🇺 Australian GP 2026 - Q1 Crash

March 7, 2026 • Melbourne

Verstappen's Red Bull locked up entering a corner at Albert Park, sending him into the barriers during Q1. He was seen shaking his wrist after impact and had an X-ray as a precaution. Martin Brundle noted it wasn't driver error — the car snapped unexpectedly.

DNQ - Barrier Impact

🇬🇧 British GP 2021 - Hamilton Contact

July 18, 2021 • Silverstone

The infamous Copse corner collision with Lewis Hamilton. Verstappen hit the barriers at 51G — one of the hardest impacts in recent F1 history. Walked away but went to hospital for precautionary checks. This intensified their championship rivalry.

51G Impact • Hospital Visit

🇮🇹 Italian GP 2021 - Sausage Kerb Launch

September 12, 2021 • Monza

Another Hamilton-Verstappen clash! Max's Red Bull rode over the sausage kerb and landed ON TOP of Hamilton's Mercedes. The halo likely saved Hamilton's life. Both out of the race. Verstappen penalized.

Car on Car • Halo Save

🇸🇦 Saudi Arabian GP 2021 - Brake Check Controversy

December 5, 2021 • Jeddah

Chaotic race with multiple restarts. Verstappen slowed to let Hamilton pass, Hamilton ran into the back of him. Accusations of "brake checking" flew. Max got penalties but finished P2. One of the most controversial races ever.

10-Second Penalty • Chaos

🇭🇺 Hungarian GP 2021 - Bottas Bowling

August 1, 2021 • Budapest

Turn 1, Lap 1 carnage. Bottas locked up and took out Norris, who then hit Verstappen. Max limped home P10 with heavy damage. Lost crucial championship points through no fault of his own.

Lap 1 Chaos • P10 Finish
